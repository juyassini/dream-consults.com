About Dream Consults

Built on passion for economic empowerment, evidence-based research, and practical solutions for Malawian businesses.

Our Story

Dream Consults emerged from a shared vision among development economics and agricultural students at the Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources (LUANAR). We recognized a critical gap in affordable, evidence-based advisory services for SMEs and agribusinesses in Malawi.

Rather than viewing Malawian businesses as isolated entities, we understand them as part of complex, interconnected value chains. Our approach combines research-backed insights with practical street wisdom to deliver actionable recommendations.

We believe sustainable economic growth comes from optimizing what businesses already do, not from chasing external solutions. Every recommendation we make is grounded in data analysis, market research, and deep understanding of the local context.
